## No
Normalization is used for mainly two purpose
 - Eliminating reduntant(useless) data.
 - Ensuring data dependencies make sense i.e data is logically stored.

 1st Normal Form
 - no two Rows of data must contain repeating group of information i.e each set of column must have a unique value

 2nd Normal Form
 - As per the Second Normal Form there must not be any partial dependency of any column on primary key.
